Dogs Red Wine

Welcome to Dogs, Red Wine, the show where we talk about everything and nothing at the same time! Each week, we'll bring you a mix of interesting interviews, segments on our favorite four-legged friends, and of course, a healthy dose of vino. So grab a glass of your favorite red and join us as we dive into the depths of the unknown and explore the uncharted territories of... well, whatever we feel like talking about. Cheers!
